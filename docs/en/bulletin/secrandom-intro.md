# Community Articles

This is where we showcase tips, tutorials, and creative uses shared by the SecScore community. Every contribution here represents real-world experience and practical wisdom.

## How to Submit Your Article

Have something to share? Here's how to submit:

1. Visit [SecScore GitHub Issues](https://github.com/SECTL/SecScore-Docs/issues)
2. Create a new Issue with the title format: `[Article] Your Article Title`
3. Include in the body:
   - Article title and brief description
   - Link to the article (if external)
   - Or paste the full article content (if contributing directly to the docs)

## What We're Looking For

- Tips and shortcuts for daily use
- Best practices for classroom management
- Creative use cases and real-world examples
- Integration workflows with other tools
- Teacher productivity optimization tips

We look forward to your contributions!
