> **View score changes and export rankings**
>
> Leaderboard summarizes score changes by time range and supports exporting to a spreadsheet for review and sharing.

## Time Range

Depending on your app version, leaderboard commonly supports:

- **Today**: quick in-class feedback
- **This Week**: weekly review
- **This Month**: monthly evaluation / stage statistics
